Home Research Design Process Final Design
  • A waste sorting mechanism that utilizes sound signatures to distinguish aluminum cans from other types of waste. The system rewards users with monetary incentives when they deposit aluminum cans, increasing recycling efficiency and accessibility, particularly in remote areas, and promoting recycling.
  • Alkan-27 was entered into the NM Governor’s STEM Challenge. The objective of the competition was to address the question, “How would you use science technology engineering and math (STEM) to make life better for your family, community, state, or country?”
  • Designed solution must be under $500.
  • Awarded state championship sponsored by Boeing.
  • Served as the lead engineer, programmer, and team manager.
