Alkan-27 has taken a simplified approach to sorting deposited items due to funding restrictions. To save time and resources during the prototype development, a microphone was used instead of a camera to analyze the items deposited in the bin as the latter was deemed too expensive and time-consuming.

Initial Sketches

Alkan-27's initial goal was to create a prototype of the device using cardboard. This would allow us to conduct testing and evaluate its effectiveness. Based on the results, we could then make necessary improvements and modifications to the device.

Finalized Prototype 1

The device has remained true to the initial sketches, with one notable addition: a removable lid to provide convenient access to the internal components.
